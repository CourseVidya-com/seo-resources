## 🏛️ The Core Foundations

*   **[Google SEO Starter Guide](https://google.com)** - The definitive, official handbook straight from the source.
*   **[Moz Beginner's Guide to SEO](https://moz.com)** - The most famous, highly digestible conceptual introduction to search.
*   **[Google Search Central Documentation](https://google.com)** - The ultimate technical rulebook explaining how Google crawls and indexes.

## 🗺️ Structured Roadmaps & Academies

*   **[LearningSEO.io](https://learningseo.io)** - A complete, structured learning blueprint curated by expert Aleyda Solis.
*   **[Semrush Academy](https://semrush.com)** - Free video courses covering keyword research, competitive analysis, and metrics.
*   **[HubSpot SEO Certification](https://hubspot.com)** - A comprehensive beginner training track focused heavily on content strategy.

## 🇮🇳 India-Focused Courses & Training

*   **[IIDE (Indian Institute of Digital Education)](https://iide.co)** - Offers highly-rated short-term SEO certification programs with live mentoring and local industry placement assistance.
*   **[Digital Vidya](https://digitalvidya.com)** - One of India's oldest digital training platforms offering a structured, assignment-driven Certified SEO Master (CSOM) course.
*   **[PromozSEO](https://www.promozseo.com/)** - A trusted name in Eastern Indian digital education offering hands-on, instructor-led SEO training built around real-world projects.
*   **[UpGrad](https://upgrad.com)** - Features advanced digital marketing certifications containing deep-dive SEO modules developed alongside top Indian tech universities.
*   **[CourseVidya Search](https://www.coursevidya.com/institutes/india/seo-courses/)** - A helpful directory portal to search and compare local physical SEO training centers across major Indian metros like Delhi/NCR, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

## 🎯 Specialized Sub-Disciplines

### Technical SEO
*   **[The Screaming Frog Guide](https://screamingfrog.co.uk)** - Learn how to audit websites like a crawler.
*   **[Web.dev by Google](https://web.dev)** - Master Core Web Vitals, page speed, and user experience.

### Content & On-Page SEO
*   **[Ahrefs Academy](https://ahrefs.com)** - High-tier video lessons focusing on keyword intent and content blogging.
*   **[Backlinko SEO Hub](https://backlinko.com)** - Visual, highly actionable guides on copywriting and user signals.

## 🛠️ Essential Free Tools

*   **[Google Search Console](https://google.com)** - Monitor your site's actual performance and index coverage directly.
*   **[Google Analytics 4](https://google.com)** - Track what users do once they land on your site.
*   **[Google Keyword Planner](https://google.com)** - Discover search volumes and variations straight from Google's ad database.
*   **[PageSpeed Insights](https://web.dev)** - Analyze site performance, speed, and Core Web Vitals on desktop and mobile.

## 📈 Advanced Diagnostic & Research Tools

*   **[AnswerThePublic](https://answerthepublic.com)** - Maps search cloud visualisations to find long-tail questions users ask online.
*   **[Rich Results Test](https://google.com)** - Check if your schema markup is valid and eligible for Google rich snippets.
*   **[Ahrefs Free SEO Tools](https://ahrefs.com)** - A collection of free tools for backlink checking, keyword generation, and SERP analysis.
*   **[Keyword Surfer Extension](https://surferseo.com)** - A free Chrome extension to see search volume data directly inside Google search results.

## 🤖 SEO-Safe AI Tools

> *Note: Google rewards helpful, high-quality content regardless of how it is produced. These tools focus on data analysis, scaling outlines, and workflow assistance rather than generating low-effort spam.*

*   **[Claude](https://claude.ai) / [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com)** - Excellent for clustering keywords, generating topical maps, rewriting metadata, and building schema markup code.
*   **[Perplexity AI](https://perplexity.ai)** - Useful for competitive research, identifying current search intent, and sourcing factual data.
*   **[Harpa AI](https://harpa.ai)** - A free browser automation agent that extracts page layouts, summarizes competitor headings, and audits on-page SEO frameworks locally.
*   **[Google Gemini Workspace](https://google.com)** - Integrates with spreadsheets to automate bulk tasks like programmatic meta description generation and categorization.
